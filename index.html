FAQ

Questions, answered.

Still have a question about your part or project? Ask our team →

How long has Alcami been manufacturing plastic parts?

Since 2001 — more than two decades of plastic injection molding and thermoforming across our factories in Thailand and the Philippines.

Which industries does Alcami serve?

We manufacture for electronics, medical and laboratory, consumer products, automotive and industrial clients — including global names such as Dyson, Western Digital, Stanley Black & Decker and Greiner Bio-One.

Which certifications does Alcami hold?

We are certified to ISO 9001 (quality), ISO 14001 (environment) and ISO 22000 (food safety), and our cleanroom manufacturing is NEBB certified.

Can I get copies of the certificates for my supplier qualification file?

Yes — we'll provide current copies of all relevant certifications for your supplier qualification file on request.

Is Alcami the right fit for low-volume or prototype work?

Absolutely. We support projects from prototype and low-volume runs through to full mass production — with in-house mold fabrication to move quickly between stages.
